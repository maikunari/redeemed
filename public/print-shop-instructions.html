Print Shop Instructions for Business Cards

Quick Overview

  • Card Type: Business cards with download codes and QR codes
  • Template: Avery 5371 Business Cards (3.5" × 2", 10 per sheet)
  • Paper: Letter size (8.5" × 11") cardstock or heavy paper (recommended: 80-120 lb)
  • Printing: Double-sided color printing
  • Output: 10 business cards per sheet

Important Printing Details

Page Setup

  • Paper Size: Letter (8.5" × 11")
  • Orientation: Portrait
  • Template: Avery 5371 or equivalent (2 columns × 5 rows)
  • No Scaling: Print at 100% - do not scale to fit page

Double-Sided Printing Instructions

The PDF contains:

  • Page 1: Front sides (with QR codes and branding)
  • Page 2: Back sides (with download codes and instructions)
For Professional Print Shops:
  1. Print Page 1 on one side of the paper
  2. Flip the paper horizontally (like turning a book page)
  3. Print Page 2 on the reverse side
  4. Cut along the guide lines

Paper Recommendations

  • Cardstock: 80-120 lb weight
  • Finish: Matte or semi-gloss (recommended)
  • Color: White or off-white background

Cutting Instructions

  • Cards are pre-positioned for Avery 5371 template
  • Standard business card size: 3.5" × 2"
  • Cut along the printed guide lines if visible
  • Use a paper cutter for cleanest results

Quality Check

Before final printing:

  • Verify QR codes are readable with a smartphone
  • Check that front and back sides align properly
  • Ensure text is crisp and colors are vibrant

Questions?

If the print shop has questions about alignment or quality, they can:

  • Print a test page first to verify alignment
  • Adjust printer settings if text appears too light/dark
  • Contact you for clarification on any special requirements
